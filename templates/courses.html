Our Courses

Undergraduate Programs

Our undergraduate programs are designed to provide students with a solid foundation in engineering and technology, equipping them with the skills to innovate and lead in their chosen fields. Below are the available UG courses:

  • Computer Science Engineering (CSE): Focuses on software development, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity.
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE): Covers topics in communication systems, signal processing, and embedded systems.
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE): Deals with electrical systems, power engineering, and renewable energy technologies.

Postgraduate Programs

Our postgraduate programs are aimed at professionals and graduates who seek advanced knowledge and skills in their fields. The following PG courses are offered:

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA): Provides insights into business management, leadership, and entrepreneurship.
  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA): Focuses on software development, data science, and advanced computing techniques.
