Placement Highlights

KLN College of Engineering has consistently achieved outstanding placement records. The placement statistics for various courses are listed below:

Postgraduate Programs

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA): 90% placement rate with leading firms like Deloitte, HDFC, and KPMG.
  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA): 89% placement rate with recruiters such as IBM, Capgemini, and Cognizant.
