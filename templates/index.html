BE - EEE


The Research and Development section is doing research on Industrial Power Harmonics and mitigation and interact with industries in measuring, recording, analyzing and designing of filters for reducing harmonics with the help of Power Quality analyzer, as per IEEE standard.

BE - ECE

The Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) Department was established in the year 1994. The department offers Undergraduate (UG), Post Graduate (PG), degree programs that provide students with the knowledge and tools they need to succeed in the Electronics and Communication Engineering. Research in the department focuses on high-impact various disciplines: Communication systems, Wireless networks, Signal and Image Processing, RF MEMS and Microwave antennas, Optical communication and Photonics, VLSI technologies.
